Public Interest Litigation

At SCP, a leading law firm with globally recognized proficiency in dispute resolution, we believe we have a responsibility to support our community in this regard. This we have consistently done through pro bono cases referred to us from State Governments, Agencies, Parastatals, the Office of the Public Defender (OPD) of Lagos State and other matters we take up on compassionate and public interest basis.

Tax Advisory

Our Tax Advisory team comprises of lawyers, with experience in advising on structuring and documenting corporate and commercial transactions for effective taxation. The team includes two fellows of the Chartered Institute of Taxation and other members with varying professional backgrounds. We provide both public and private sector perspectives on local and cross-border transactions. The team provides top-notch advisory services on all aspects of tax including review of applicable legal/tax regime, review of corporate structures and business entry strategies, tax compliance audit, interface with tax authorities, investigation of tax evasion and avoidance strategies, recovery of unremitted taxes, dispute resolution between tax authorities and tax payers and provision of in-house training for tax personnel.

Private Equity and Venture Capital

At SCP, we understand that success requires not only technical legal skills, but also commercial sophistication, common sense, top-quality project management and the ability to communicate effectively. Our Private Equity team prides itself on having these attributes, as well as a wealth of experience in structuring and executing joint ventures, institutional and management-led buyouts, mezzanine financing, public-to-privates, follow-on acquisitions, IPOs and trade sales. Areas of expertise include traditional private equity, alternative asset funds and hedge funds. We provide advice and representation for private equity firms, venture capitalists, fund sponsors and institutional investors on all aspects of private equity transactions – from the establishment and structuring of new funds, to operations, acquisitions and dispositions, to the final exit planning and liquidation.

Real Estate

Businesses and society cannot function without the services of commercial property. The commercial property sector is a fundamental aspect of economic growth through the provision of space for a growing urban population. At SCP, our real estate practice involves a varied and often highly complex set of activities that range from negotiation, sales, acquisition, title, land use to litigation. From the real estate investor to the buyer, our professionalism, integrity, accountability and quality service, often results in maximum return on investment. SCP leverages on its relationship with key government agencies to nurture real estate transactions from beginning to end.

AVIATION

The aviation sector has become very important not only as a means of transportation but for the purpose of facilitating businesses both locally and internationally. Our aviation practice group ranges from advisory services to air transport providers who intend to enter the Nigerian airspace for business, regulatory consultancy services to aviation regulators concerned about compliance with safety regulations, to claims for damages and losses suffered by both air service providers and air travelers. We also advise on development and concession of critical infrastructure at air travel facilities and maintenance services.

Public Sector & Regulatory

Engaging the public sector is probably the most important component in initiating and successfully concluding transactions in the developing world. This applies to Nigeria’s current developmental context. A vital part of our skills and abilities in our public sector practice is providing informed and experienced representation to and on behalf of our clients at government (Executive and or Legislative) processes

SCP continues to provide experienced counsel to individuals, corporate entities and government institutions, in intricate and sensitive issues of law and regulation. SCP currently advises several State Governments and regulatory agencies in significant matters regarding taxation, public health, public revenues, et al. Our Public Sector and Regulatory Group designs effective strategies and deploys its vast experience, knowledge and relationships for each engagement in the engagement.
