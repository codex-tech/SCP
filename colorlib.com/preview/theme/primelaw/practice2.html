Our investigation and inquiry practice team provide dedicated investigations and inquiries services. Banking and Finance

We possess deep understanding of the yearnings of Clients in the Banking and Financial sector. Dispute Resolution

Understanding the uncertainties of the Nigerian business environment,the risks on investments and returns, and a changing regulatory landscape are key to a vibrant dispute resolution law firm.At SCP,we identify and assess client's legal risks and liabilities before they arise.Where such disputes occur,we assist in devising and implementing the right strategy.Where arbitration, mediation, or other forms of ADR are the best approach,our lawyers rank among the world's best.Where litigation is required,our lawyers possess experience and knowledge to advocate aggressively and incisively for clients.
