Banking and finance

Providing tailor-made advisory services to our Clients to aid them in navigating the numerous banking regulatory requirements has been our age long practice. At SCP, we provide an array of legal services to financial institutions, lenders and borrowers, individuals and corporate clients as well as financial regulators. Our hallmark is always hinged on our approach to navigating the complexities in providing practical and applicable solutions in every transaction.

Dispute Resolution

Understanding the dynamics of the Nigerian business environment, investment risks, and the changing regulatory landscape is key to a vibrant dispute resolution Law Firm. At SCP, we identify and assess the client's legal risks and liabilities before they arise. Where such disputes occur, we assist in devising and implementing the right strategy. Whether what is required is arbitration, mediation, or other forms of ADR, or litigation, our lawyers are equipped to give the right representation to our Clients.

Investigation and Inquiry Services

Company Overview

We know about the Law

The firm’s regulatory and public policy practice cuts across the many disciplines that require highly specialized knowledge and experience to address governmental regulation of the private markets. Our leading regulatory practice has seen us represent a diversified client base in various sectors including gas, antitrust, energy, telecommunications, environmental, capital market, food and drug, State finance, government contracts and international trade.
Many of our practice leaders as well as our associates have governmental experience that enhances their ability to serve SCP clients in regulatory fields, while others have held prominent positions in regulated industries. In addition, our public policy practice is acknowledged as among the largest and most influential in Nigeria and affords our clients the opportunity to shape regulatory and legislative initiatives affecting their businesses.
